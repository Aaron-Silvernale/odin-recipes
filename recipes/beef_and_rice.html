Easy Beef and Rice

Beef and Rice

This is a nutrient dense shake that tastes like death but you feel good after. Its like a cold plunge in a shake!

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • corn
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of grated ginger
  • spinach
  • rice
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Brown meat in dish
  2. cook rice
  3. add veggies to meat
  4. in small bowl mix soy sauce, ginger, and garlic
  5. add sauce mixture to beef
  6. serve beef over the rice
