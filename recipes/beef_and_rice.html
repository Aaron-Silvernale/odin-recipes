Easy Beef and Rice

Beef and Rice

Simple, quick, easy, and tastes good. What else could you want? An egg in your beer?

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • corn
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of grated ginger
  • spinach
  • rice
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Brown meat in dish
  2. cook rice
  3. add veggies to meat
  4. in small bowl mix soy sauce, ginger, and garlic
  5. add sauce mixture to beef
  6. serve beef over the rice
