Homemade Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious homemade lasagna recipe that is easy to make

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • meat
  • onion
  • bell peppers
  • egg plant
  • ricotta
  • cottage cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. boil water
  3. brown meat
  4. set aside
  5. fry onion, garlic, and bell peppers
  6. In a seperate bowl mix together ricotta and cottage cheese
  7. Add noodles to boiling water
  8. When noodles are al dente, remove and drain
  9. Mix browned meat and veggies with tomato sauce
  10. Put a thin layer of sauce on bottom of dish, then layer on noodles, sauce, and cheese. Repeating until dish is full
  11. Cook in oven for 45 minutes
  12. enjoy!
