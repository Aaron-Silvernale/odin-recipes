Hulk Load Shake Recipe

This is a nutrient dense shake that tastes like death but you feel good after. Its like a cold plunge in a shake!

Ingredients

  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 bunch kale
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • lemon juice
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 hand of peeled ginger
  • 1 apple

Steps

  1. Add a cup of water to a blender
  2. Add celery, lemon juice and half the kale
  3. pulse blender a couple times to break it down
  4. add the rest of the kale and pulse again
  5. add apple (cored)
  6. add in garlic and ginger and pulse
  7. blend until drinkable
  8. Drink it before it quagulates
